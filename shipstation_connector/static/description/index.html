SEO FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for common buyer questions and Odoo Apps search intent.

What is the ShipStation Odoo Connector?

It is an Odoo module that connects Odoo with ShipStation to sync orders, products, customers, inventory, shipments, labels, tracking details, reports, and logs.

Does it support Odoo to ShipStation sync?

Yes. It supports Odoo to ShipStation sync for orders, products, inventory, categories, attributes, and shipment-related workflows.

Does it support ShipStation to Odoo sync?

Yes. It can bring ShipStation orders, customers, shipment updates, label-related details, tracking numbers, and fulfillment records into Odoo.

Can I sync tracking numbers with Odoo?

Yes. Shipment tracking numbers and carrier details can be synchronized back into Odoo for better shipment visibility.

Does it support multiple ShipStation accounts?

Yes. Multi-instance support allows businesses to manage multiple ShipStation accounts or fulfillment environments from Odoo.

Is this connector compatible with Odoo 18?

Yes. This module is designed for Odoo 18 and supports Community and Enterprise deployments.
